Space Sprouts? Noice!

COZY COSMIC CHAOS

Launch into a chaotic spaceship journey where everything is simulated. Bend the rules of physics, experiment with unusual gadgets, toss everything around, and discover hidden corners aboard your solar punk spaceship with each replay.

  • A curious physical 2D sandbox on board a solar punk spaceship.

  • A loop-based experience full of secrets and surprises.

  • A game full of cozy cosmic chaos that cant be played wrong.
