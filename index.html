What is
Space Sprouts? Noice!

COZY COSMIC CHAOS

Launch into a chaotic spaceship adventure: bend the rules of physics, experiment with unusual gadgets and toss everything around. With each journey, you will have limited time to reveal the mysteries aboard your solar punk spaceship - how much will you discover before you arrive home?

  • A curious physical 2D sandbox on board a solar punk spaceship.

  • A loop-based experience full of secrets and surprises.

  • A game full of cozy cosmic chaos that can't be played wrong.
